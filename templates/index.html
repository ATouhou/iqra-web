Iqra

Recite an ayah or a single word, and watch as Iqra uses voice recognition and custom algorithms to find the matches in the Quran!

Current Features:

  • Recognizes recitation of full verses, partial verses, and even single words
  • Provides all matches for verses repeated in the Quran as well as a result count
  • Supports providing matches in Arabic as well as in an English translation or transliteration
  • Allows results to easily be shared
  • Available in English and Arabic

Contact:

This project is developed and maintained on a volunteer basis for the sake of the Ummah. If you'd like to provide feedback or learn how you can contribute, send an email to info@iqraapp.com.
